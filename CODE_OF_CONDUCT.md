# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to be respectful, constructive, and professional in all project spaces.

## Expected Behavior

- Use inclusive and respectful language.
- Focus on technical issues, not personal attacks.
- Accept feedback and collaborate in good faith.
- Report security concerns responsibly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Trolling, insults, or inflammatory comments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Sustained disruption of project discussions

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or edit contributions and communication that violate this policy and may
temporarily or permanently restrict participation for repeated or severe violations.

## Reporting

Report conduct violations to the maintainers via the contact guidance in `SECURITY.md` or `SUPPORT.md`.
